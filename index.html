About Us

Mission

At JCIMA-TravelExperiences we are dedicated to providing authentic and enriching travel experiences that connect our clients with the incredible cultural and natural diversity of Ecuador. We are committed to offering personalized high-quality service while promoting sustainable and responsible tourism.

Vision

We aspire to be recognized as the leading company in specialized tourism experiences in Ecuador, standing out for excellence in service, commitment to sustainability and positive impact on local communities. We seek to expand our operations while always maintaining the authenticity and quality that characterize us.

History

JCIMA-TravelExperiences was born from the passion to showcase Ecuador's wonders to the world. Founded by a team of tourism enthusiasts with extensive experience in the sector, our company has grown thanks to the dedication to creating memorable and authentic experiences. We are proud to work directly with local communities to offer our clients genuine cultural immersion while contributing to the sustainable development of these regions.
